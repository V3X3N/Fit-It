# Fit-It

**Fit-It** is a Kotlin Multiplatform application designed to help users monitor their nutrition, track water intake, and develop healthier eating habits. The app shares core logic between Android and desktop platforms, ensuring consistent functionality across devices.

## ✨ Features

- **Calendar View**  
  Browse and add daily entries for meals and water consumption.

- **Meal Segmentation**  
  Organize your day into distinct meals with customizable calorie targets.

- **Meal Summary & Macros**  
  View a summary of your meals, including total calories and a macronutrient breakdown (proteins, fats, carbohydrates).

- **Custom Calorie Limit**  
  Set and manage your personal daily calorie intake goal.

- **Personal Food Database**  
  Create and maintain your own database of food products for accurate tracking.

- **Portion-Based Logging**  
  Log the exact weight (in grams) of the food consumed for precise nutritional data.

- **Water Intake Tracker**  
  Record daily water intake to support proper hydration habits.

## 🛠 Technology Stack

- **Kotlin Multiplatform** – Shared business logic for Android and desktop
- **Jetpack Compose** – Declarative UI framework used on both Android and desktop

## 🖥 Target Platforms

- Android
- iOS *(planned)*
- Desktop *(Windows/macOS – depending on final configuration)*
